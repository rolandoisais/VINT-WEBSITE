Regulatory Alert

FSMA 204 is coming.
Paper systems won't survive it.

The FDA's FSMA Section 204 traceability rule requires food manufacturers to maintain detailed traceability records and deliver them to the FDA within 24 hours of a request. For US exporters in Latin America, this is not optional — it is the price of market access. The compliance deadline is July 20, 2028. VINT is built for exactly this requirement.
